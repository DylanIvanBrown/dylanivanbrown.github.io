## What is RSS?

RSS is a simple, open format that lets you subscribe to websites without giving anyone your email address. Your RSS reader checks for new posts and surfaces them all in one place -- no algorithm, no ads, no tracking. Just the posts.

If you don't have an RSS reader yet, [Feedly](https://feedly.com) is a good free option to start with.
